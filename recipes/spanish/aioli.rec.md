Aioli for 4
===========

Lovely creamy garlicy sauce

    2 cloves garlic
    0.25tsp coarse salt
    0.5cup mayonnaise
    2tbsp olive oil
    1tbsp lemon juice

    mix (
        mash to a paste in small bowl (
            garlic,
            coarse salt
        ),
        mayonnaise,
        olive oil,
        lemon juice
    )
