Plain White Bread for 2
=======================

    1kg of plain strong white flour
    650ml of water, room temperature
    1tsp salt
    2tbsp sugar
    1 sachet of yeast

    bake 20min(
        rise 1hour(
            kneed(
            knock air out of bread(
                    rise 1hour(
                        kneed until pastry like(
                            plain strong white flour,
                            water,
                            salt,
                            sugar,
                            yeast))))))
