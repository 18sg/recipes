Fuccacia for 2
==============

A bread recipe from Salman (aljamms8@cs.man.ac.uk)

    500g plain white flour
    400ml water
    2tsp salt
    4tbsp olive oil
    2 sachet of yeast

    bake 20min(
        sprinkle(
            poke dents on surface with wet fingers(
                rise 1hour(
                    roll on baking sheet(
                        rise till doubled in size(
                            kneed 10-15mins(
                                plain white flour,
                                water,
                                salt,
                                olive oil,
                                sachet of yeast))))),
        extra olive oil,
        coarse salt,
        herbs or onion or tomato))
