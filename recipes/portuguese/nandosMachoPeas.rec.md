Nando's Macho Peas for 4
========================

Nando's recipe from their instagram

    400g frozen peas
    water, boil
    1tbsp olive oil
    1tbsp butter
    4 sprigs fresh parsley, chop finely
    3 sprigs fresh mint, chop finely
    0.25tsp salt
    0.5tsp red chilli flakes

    mix(
        drain(
            cook 3mins(
                water,
                frozen peas
            ),
        ),
        cook 30sec(
            melt in drained pan(
                olive oil,
                butter
            ),
            salt,
            red chilli flakes
        ),
        sprigs fresh parsley,
        sprigs fresh mint
    )
