Orange Soda Syrup

Dilute with still or sparkling water like a cordial

1.36kg sugar
57g citric acid
4 oranges, zest
3 lemons, juice
2.3l water, boil

	stand for >24h(
		mix(

				mix until disolved(
					sugar,
					oranges,
					citric acid,
					water
				)
			),
			juice of oranges,
			lemons
		)
	)
)
