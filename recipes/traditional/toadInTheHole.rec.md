Toad in the Hole for 4
======================

    8 sausages
    1tbsp oil
    225g plain flour
    4 eggs
    125ml milk 1
    125ml milk 2

    ladle over sausages bake 35 min(
        gradually mix in(
            whisk together(
                plain flour, eggs, milk 1),
            milk 2),
        bake 10 mins(
            preheat oven 200,
            pour over bottom of baking dish(oil),
            sausages))
