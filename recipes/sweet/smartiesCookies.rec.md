Smarties Cookies

Easy to make and very tasty! Substitute Smarties for any other chocolate you
fancy!

100g brown sugar
100g butter
150g self-raising flour
1 tbsp golden syrup
85g smarties

cool on wire rack (
	bake for 12 mins (
		preheat oven 180*c,
		place teaspoon balls on baking tray (
			mix (
				golden syrup,
				self-raising flour,
				smarties,
				cream (butter, brown sugar)))))
