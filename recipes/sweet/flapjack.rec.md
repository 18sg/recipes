Martin's Flapjack
=================

A recipe for flapjack a friend of ours makes which is excellent when he makes
it. It may not be if we do though so no promisses.

    6oz margarine
    6oz sugar
    1tbsp golden syrup
    12oz porridge oats
    1oz self raising flour

    cool completely (
        cut edges of pieces (
            cook 18 min (
                preheat oven 180c,
                mix(
                    melt(margarine, sugar, golden syrup),
                    porridge oats,
                    self raising flour))))
