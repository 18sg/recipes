Rescaled from 2 servings.

This recipe has always seemed to produce especially good roast potatoes for Jonathan. It makes a fair quantity so the 'for 1' here assumes you are serious about wanting a lot of potatoes...

You can almost certainly omit the Rosemary and Garlic for a fairly robust 'every day' roast potato recipe -- but you really shouldn't! Likewise, you can chop the potatoes smaller or larger to taste.

You can do all of the steps up to the potatoes going into the oven the day before. Just cover and keep in the fridge.

preheat fan oven to 170°c roast 30 minutes squash slightly with potato masher coat roast for 40-45 more minutes
3 medium to large potatoes peel cut into approx 6cm pieces boil for 7 minutes drain and steam in colander for 3 minutes shake in colander until all surfaces are roughed up coat and arrange on baking tray
oil
1 cloves of garlic minced mix and allow to stand for a while
